Instructions

Welcome to our visualization of political donations in the United States. Here, you can explore how political entities transfer money from 1980-2018.

Interactive Components

  • Pick an election year to consider using the slider at the top of the page.
  • You can search for a particular candidate or committee using the search feature. Type in at least three characters to select from the drop-down list.
  • You can also re-center the graph by clicking on a node.
  • Not every contribution in the dataset is shown, just the top contributions. The degree of each node is limited to 30.

Data Source: Federal Election Commission (FEC) (last updated on 26-10-2020)

Authors: Aviva Prins, Ryan Sullivan, Sai Yerrameddy, Tanya Shroff
